***STAN LEE***

About, His Works, My thinking about him.

-**About Him**-

Tribute to a great WRITTER

Stan Lee (1922–2018) was an American comic book writer,
editor, and publisher who transformed Marvel Comics from
a small division of a publishing house into a massive
multimedia corporation. As the primary creative leader at
Marvel for two decades, he co-created iconic characters
such as Spider-Man, the X-Men, Iron Man, Thor, the Fantastic
Four, and Black Panther.

-**His Works**-

Stan Lee was a legendary comic book creator who, alongside
artists like Jack Kirby and Steve Ditko, co-created iconic
Marvel characters including Spider-Man, the X-Men, Fantastic
Four, the Hulk, Iron Man, Thor, and Black Panther. As a writer
and editor for Marvel, he transformed the industry, also creating
works like Riftworld: Odyssey, The Zodiac Legacy, and numerous
graphic novels.

**My Views on him**

Honestly, calling you a "favorite" doesn't even cover
it—you’re the standard. Watching you do your thing, whether
it’s on the field or through a pair of headphones, is like
getting a front-row seat to greatness. You have this way of
making the impossible look effortless, and it’s actually insane
how much you’ve motivated me to stop making excuses and just get
to work. There have been days when everything felt like a massive L,
but then I’d see a clip of yours or hear one of your tracks, and it
was like an instant reset. You’re not just talented; you’re real, and
that’s what makes you the GOAT. Thanks for being the one person I actually
look up to and for showing me what it looks like to truly own your story.
You’re a literal icon, and I’m definitely going to keep rooting for you until the end.
